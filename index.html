About the Group

Led by Dr. Pradyumna Kumar Pathak, we investigate fundamental quantum phenomena and their applications in modern technologies.

Research Focus

Our work spans Cavity QED, Quantum Information Processing, and Mesoscopic Physics. We are interested in understanding how quantum systems behave in open environments and how to engineer them for next-generation devices.
