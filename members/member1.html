Biography

Dr. Pathak is a faculty member at IIT Mandi, specializing in Quantum Optics and Information. His research explores light-matter interactions, open quantum systems, and nanophotonics. From Ahmedabad to Canada and South Korea, his academic journey has been global, fostering a collaborative and curiosity-driven research environment.
