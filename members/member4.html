Biography

Fun Fact: Ah my RAM is low!

Hi all! This is Mech, currently a first-year PhD student. I come from a small village on a riverine island in Assam (Majuli). Starting from zero, I never stopped dreaming. I dreamt of a campus by a river, surrounded by hills. So here I am!

I am still exploring where to head in terms of a main PhD problem. As a starting point, however, I am working on Cavity QED systems. I am very fond of the measurement problem, decoherence phenomena, and how these can be used as resources. In parallel, I am exploring what Quantum Thermodynamics has to offer to my interests.
