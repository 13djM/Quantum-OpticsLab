Teaching

Dr. Pathak teaches various undergraduate and graduate courses in Physics at IIT Mandi.

PH 511: Quantum Mechanics I

Semester: Autumn 2024

A comprehensive introduction to the principles of quantum mechanics, covering wave mechanics, matrix mechanics, and angular momentum.

PH 612: Quantum Optics

Semester: Spring 2025

Advanced course on the quantization of the electromagnetic field, coherence theory, and light-matter interactions.

PH 101: Introduction to Physics

Semester: Autumn 2023

Fundamental physics course for first-year undergraduate students.
