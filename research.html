Research Highlights

Dr. Pathak's research program investigates the fundamental physics of cavity quantum electrodynamics and solid-state quantum photonics. We explore the intricate dynamics of light-matter interaction, providing theoretical frameworks that may underpin future scalable quantum information processing architectures.
